## About

I'm an Electrical Engineer from the **Colorado School of Mines**, currently pursuing my **Master of Science in Electrical Engineering**, with an expected graduation date of **May 2027**.

My interests lie at the intersection of:

- Signal Processing
- Wireless Communications
- Antenna Systems
- RF Engineering
- Digital Signal Processing

I conduct research in the **Antennas, RFID, and Computational Electromagnetics (ARC) Laboratory**, where I work on phased-array systems, beamforming, and spatiotemporally varying electromagnetic structures. Alongside research, I've gained industry experience developing satellite communication and DSP systems through internships at **Rincon Research Corporation** and **Zeta Associates**.

Throughout my academic career, I've been honored with multiple **Dean's List** recognitions and received the **Outstanding Graduating Senior Award** from the Electrical Engineering Department.
